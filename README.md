# Individual Case Write-Ups

## Project Overview

This repository contains completed individual case write-ups for the course **Organizational Behavior, Leadership, and Sustainability**.

The write-ups focus on analyzing real business and leadership cases using concepts from organizational behavior, leadership, and sustainability.

## Assignment Description

For this assignment, each student completed two individual case write-ups during the semester. Each write-up analyzes one selected case and presents a structured reflection on the main issues, possible solutions, and managerial implications.

Each write-up follows three main sections:

1. **Analysis of the Problem(s)**
   - Identification of the key organizational or leadership challenges
   - Explanation of the causes behind the problem
   - Connection to relevant course concepts

2. **Recommendations**
   - Proposed solutions to the identified problems
   - Practical actions that managers or leaders could take
   - Justification of the recommendations

3. **Implications**
   - Expected effects of the recommendations
   - Possible risks, limitations, or unintended consequences
   - Broader lessons for leadership and organizational behavior

## Topics Covered

The write-ups may cover topics such as:

- Leadership style
- Organizational structure
- Motivation
- Team decision-making
- Change management
- Power and influence
- Managing relationships
- Sustainability leadership
- Stakeholder management

## Deliverables

The completed work includes:

- Individual Case Write-Up #1
- Individual Case Write-Up #2
- Final submitted PDF or Word files
